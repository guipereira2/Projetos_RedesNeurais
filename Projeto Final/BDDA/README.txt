The resolution of the camera videos is 720x1280. The resolution of the gaze map videos is 768x1024. The gaze map videos contain unuseful black bars on the top and bottom of the frame.
So the central region of 576x1024 pixels of each gaze map video corresponds to the whole frame of the camera video.

The gps json files contain contain six columns. The unit of the course is degree. The unit of the timestamp is millisecond. The unit of the speed is m/s.
